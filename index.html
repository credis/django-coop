django-coop

An work-in-progress to build collaborative organizations management server for the Social and Solidarity Economy Sector. Django-coop manages organizations, contact informations, members, relations between organizations, places, impact areas, tags, categories, exchanges (products, services, partnerships). It feeds selected data through a D2R server to a RDF aggregation platform that can acts as a hub for different networks.

Installation intructions are (in french...) on the wiki.
